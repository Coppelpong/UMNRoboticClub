About Ultrasonic Sensor

An ultrasonic sensor is a device that measures distance by emitting ultrasonic sound waves and detecting the time it takes for the sound waves to reflect back from an object. It is widely used in robotics, automation, and obstacle detection systems. Ultrasonic sensors can measure distances up to several meters with high accuracy. They are particularly useful in environments where other distance sensors, such as infrared, may struggle due to lighting conditions. Applications include robotics navigation, vehicle parking systems, and industrial automation processes. Additionally, ultrasonic sensors are known for their reliability and ability to work in dusty or dark environments without interference.
